The Rules

1. The photo is your life

A laminated photo of you is taped to your upper back. If your assassin snatches it, you are dead.

2. One secret target

When you make a kill, this site automatically reassigns you their target.

3. The kill

Snatch your target's photo off their back in one clean grab.

No pushing, charging, or hitting.

4. Defence

You may dodge, turn, or walk away.

There are no calls. No timeouts. No immunity. If you see them coming, move.

5. Alliances — at your own risk

You may team up with strictly one other person.

Alliances are informal. The game does not recognise them and does not protect them.

Only the assigned assassin's snatch counts.

Remember: the chain is a loop. Your ally may be the person assigned to kill you.

6. The map

In play: Levels 1, 4, 14 and 24.

Out of bounds: the pool area. Nobody goes near the water.

7. Lifts

No kills inside. No camping the lift or its doors. No hiding in the lift.

It's for travelling between floors — use it and move on.

8. The endgame

At 5:20, all surviving players must return to the function room and remain there.

All targets dissolve. Everyone hunts everyone. Any photo is fair game.

The last photo standing wins.

This will be the death of you.....
