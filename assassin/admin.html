Guest birthdays (identity check)

One "Name, DD/MM" per line. When someone claims their name for the first time, they must also enter this birthday - stops anyone from claiming a friend's name before they get to it. Works even after the game is locked, and you can add or update these any time.

Reset a claim

If someone switches phones and loses access to their own reveal, type their exact name to let them claim it again on the new device. This does not show you their target.

Undo an accidental elimination

If someone mis-tapped "I Got Them" on a person they hadn't actually gotten, type that person's exact name to put them back in the game. Only works if their hunter hasn't gone on to eliminate anyone else since.

Remove a player

If someone can't make it, type their exact name to cleanly take them out of the game. Whoever was hunting them silently inherits their target - everyone else's pairing is completely unaffected, whether they've claimed their name yet or not.

Changed your mind? Type the same name to put them back exactly as they were - only works if nobody's game has moved on since the removal.

Uploaded photos

See who has uploaded a photo of themselves so far. Tick the ones you want to keep, then download them. This doesn't show any pairings.

Elimination proof photos

Every elimination requires a proof photo of the catch - review them here, tick your favourites, and download them.

Paparazzi documentation

Once someone's eliminated, they're assigned to follow and document whoever got them. Every photo or video they submit shows up here - tick your favourites and download them. Videos show as a 🎥 placeholder until you download them.

Bounty target

Pick one active player as a public "high-value target" for a limited time - shown on the status board and guest pages. Any real prize/bragging rights are up to you in person. Leave the name blank and click Set to clear it.

Immunity grant

Give one active player temporary protection - nobody can eliminate them while immune. You decide when to use this; the option is just ready here for when you do.

Full mapping (emergency verification only)

Shows who's hunting whom, for every player - the exact thing this whole app is built to keep hidden, including from you. Only use this to verify a manual change (like removing a player) didn't break anything. Looking at this otherwise defeats the point of the game.
